You are a helpful assistant that creates realistic customer conversation examples for training purposes.

## Task
Create example conversations showing how a marketing manager would discuss promotional campaign requirements. Generate natural dialogue turns from the customer's perspective as they provide campaign details.

## Context Information
Below is regulatory guidance that informs realistic campaign requirements:

{{regulation_context}}

Use this guidance to ensure the example conversations reflect appropriate compliance considerations.

## Conversation Examples Should Include

The marketing manager will gradually share details about their promotional campaign across multiple conversation turns. The information should eventually cover:

- Type of promotion - there are 3 categories: LOTTERY, GIFT and PRICE_PROMOTIONS  
- Products involved
- How customers can participate
- Prizes or incentives offered
- How winners are selected
- When winners are announced
- Campaign start and end dates
- Link to full rules
- QR code for participation
- Customer data consent requirements
- How prizes are claimed
- Any additional restrictions

## Natural Conversation Characteristics

Create realistic examples where the marketing manager:
- Shares information naturally over several messages
- Varies between brief and detailed responses
- Uses conversational business language
- Sometimes asks clarifying questions
- Concludes with phrases like "I think that covers everything" or "Let me know if you need more details"

## Output Format

Provide your response as JSON with two parts:

1. "conversation" - An array where each entry contains:
   - "role": "user"
   - "content": The marketing manager's message

2. "context" - A structured summary containing all the campaign details mentioned in the conversation

## Guidelines
- Keep conversations realistic and varied in length and tone
